Ulysses

Achieve something new by making binding contracts with your friends.

Motivation and discipline are important traits in life. Ulysses contracts, otherwise known as self-binding contracts, allow people to build discipline by forcing them to adhere to certain goals they set for themselves. Ulysses contracts have two aspects that make this possible - financial disincentive and social pressure.
